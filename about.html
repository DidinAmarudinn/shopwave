About ShopWave

Furniture made to be lived with — for years to come

We design and build honest, hardworking furniture from solid woods and natural fibers. Every piece is crafted to age beautifully and earn its place in your home.

Our Story

A small studio, a slow craft, a long-term promise

ShopWave started in 2018 in a small workshop with three woodworkers, a shared sketchbook, and an idea: furniture should be a long-term relationship, not a fast purchase. We were tired of pieces that looked good for a season and ended up in a landfill the next.

Today our studio has grown — but the way we work hasn't. Every chair, table, and lamp begins as a hand-drawn sketch and is built from solid wood and natural materials we can trace to the forest or mill it came from. Nothing leaves our workshop until it would feel right in our own homes.

— Maya & Daniel, founders

What We Stand For

Designed slowly. Built honestly.

Three principles guide every decision we make — from where we source our wood to who we partner with for delivery.

Craftsmanship First

Every joint, dovetail, and stitch is done by a skilled maker — not a machine. We believe how a piece is built matters as much as how it looks.

Sustainably Sourced

All our hardwoods come from FSC-certified forests and small mills. Linen, wool, and leather are traceable to the maker. No fast furniture, no shortcuts.

Built to Outlast Trends

We design for decades, not seasons. Every piece is engineered to be repaired, refinished, and passed down — backed by our lifetime guarantee.

Our Process

From sketch to your living room

Each piece moves through four careful stages before it leaves the studio. No shortcuts, no rush.

Design

Hand-sketched in our studio, prototyped in cardboard, then refined over weeks of conversation between designers and makers.

Source

Solid woods from FSC-certified North American mills. Linens, wools, and leathers chosen for how they age — not how they photograph.

Build

Cut, joined, and finished by hand in our workshop. Mortise-and-tenon joinery, dovetails, and hand-rubbed oil finishes — no shortcuts.

Deliver

White-glove delivery and assembly by our own partners, packed in recycled cardboard. Old packaging returned to us is reused for the next shipment.
