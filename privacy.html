Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

ShopWave ("we", "our", or "us") operates a demo e-commerce website. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our site.

Demo Store Disclaimer

ShopWave is a demonstration website with dummy product data. No real purchases, payments, or order fulfillment take place on this site.

Information We Collect

As a static demo site, ShopWave does not require account registration or collect personal information directly. However, standard web hosting services (such as GitHub Pages) may automatically collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring website addresses
  • General geographic location (country/region level)

Cookies

ShopWave itself does not set cookies. Third-party services used for hosting or fonts may use cookies or similar technologies. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Our site loads product images from external image providers (e.g., Unsplash) and fonts from Google Fonts. These services have their own privacy policies governing their data practices.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure our demo site is served over HTTPS. Since no personal data is actively collected, there is no user data stored by ShopWave.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@shopwave-demo.example.
